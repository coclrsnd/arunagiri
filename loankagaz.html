VIDYATM

Customized ERP Solutions for Educational Excellence

Vidyatm is Arunagiri Infotech's premier ERP software, tailored specifically for educational institutions. Designed to streamline administrative processes, enhance operational efficiency, and support academic excellence, Vidyatm has been trusted by numerous reputed institutions.

Key Features of Vidyatm:

  • Student Management

    Manage student profiles, attendance, progress tracking, and performance analysis effortlessly.

  • Faculty Management

    Simplify faculty scheduling, payroll, workload distribution, and evaluation procedures.

  • Academic Administration

    Control course structures, timetables, exams, grading, and academic compliance effectively.

  • Finance Management

    Streamline fee collection, budgeting, accounting, and financial reporting with precision.

  • Communication Tools

    Enable seamless communication between students, faculty, and parents through centralized messaging.
