PARENT APP

A white-labeled mobile application designed to enhance communication and engagement between schools and parents.

In addition to our comprehensive ERP solutions, Arunagiri Infotech offers a fully white-labeled Android application designed exclusively for parents. This app, customized with your institution’s branding, strengthens the relationship between the school and parents through seamless communication and real-time updates.

Key Features of the Parent App:

  • School Branding

    Customized to reflect your institution’s identity, creating a cohesive, professional, and branded user experience.

  • Real-Time Updates

    Instant notifications about attendance, marks, homework, announcements, and more.

  • Communication

    Enables parents and teachers to communicate directly, supporting student development through collaboration.

  • Access to Information

    Easy access to timetables, exam schedules, events, circulars, and school activities.

  • Secure and Confidential

    Implements robust security protocols to ensure complete safety of personal and academic information.

With our white-labeled Parent App, families remain actively connected to the school community. Empower parents with a modern, intuitive, and secure mobile solution designed to improve communication and support student success.
